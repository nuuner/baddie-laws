Laws of Ascension

as·cend·ed

/əˈsɛndɪd/

A self-assured individual who commands attention through composure rather than noise. Although they do not seek approval, their autonomy creates a natural gravitational pull; others actively seek their proximity and emulate their behavior. They set firm boundaries without explanation. Their value is established by their results, not their effort.

L1
Be Confident in Everything You Do Eliminate hesitation; a bold mistake is better than a timid success.
L2
Make It Seem Effortless Conceal your labor; let the world see the result, never the sweat.
L3
Offset Dorkiness with Ascended Behavior Humanize your power; use relatability to shield yourself from envy.
L4
Always Say Less Than Necessary Master the silence; let others reveal themselves while you remain a mystery.
L5
Re-create Yourself Command your narrative; don't inherit an identity, curate one.
L6
Be Royal in Your Own Fashion Demand high standards; treat yourself like a prize to be treated like one.
